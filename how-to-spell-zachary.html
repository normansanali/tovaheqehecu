What is the correct way to spell Zach?

Zack (and variant spellings Zach, Zac, Zak, Zakk) is sometimes a given name, but more often it is a hypocorism or short form of another given name, usually Zachary in the English speaking world, which derives from Zechariah.

Is Zach or Zack more common?

Zach is a shortened form of Zacharia(s), from the Bible. The name is also translated and spelled or used differently in other languages. (Zachary, Zack, Zak, etc.). The modern and easy way currently popular is Zak or Zac.
