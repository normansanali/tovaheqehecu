The Car Collection of Drew Brees

Drew Christopher Brees (born January 15, 1979) is a former NFL quarterback who played for the New Orleans Saints. Brees was selected by the San Diego Chargers at the 2001 NFL Draft. After joining the saints, Drew was named Offensive Player of the Year and led the NFL passing yards 7 times. Brees earns more than $30 million annually and has an estimated net worth of a massive $180 million. Let’s look at what cool cars the NFL star Drew Brees has got inside his expensive car collection.

1. Bugatti Veyron – $2 Million

Drew Brees has got a Bugatti Veyron in his extensive car collection. The Veyron a masterpiece of modern art and a result of automotive excellence. The supercar crossed every boundary of innovation and challenged the laws of physics with its mammoth power. It featured a super aerodynamic design made from outside to inside just to keep the car stable at high speeds. Bugatti made a one-of-a-kind 8.0L quad-turbo W16 engine that made 1,200 horsepower. That ginormous power delivery helped it to rocket from 0-60 mph in under 2.46 seconds with a top speed of 254 mph.

2. Rolls-Royce Cullinan – $340,000

Next comes the Rolls-Royce Cullinan, which Drew Brees has got in his expensive car collection. It is the Rolls’ approach of providing the brand’s best luxury and silent performance packed inside an SUV body. The Cullinan features a fluid design combined with an ultra-rich cabin fitted with rich materials. The automaker has offered the best of its craftsmanship packed into a high-riding practical design. The Cullinan offers a super-refined 6.7L twin-turbo V12 engine that makes 563 hp offering a buttery smooth drive.

3. Mercedes-AMG GT-R – $160,000

Drew Brees has also got a Mercedes-AMG GT-R inside his expensive car collection. The GT-R provides the Mercedes’ best amount of thrill and comes at half the price of an Aventador. The beast rocks a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine that shells out up to 720 hp and over 590 lb-ft of torque. The GT-R is built only to satisfy the motorheads with its mystique performance and racing dynamics. The car can rocket from 0-60 mpg in just 3.3 seconds and can achieve a top speed of 198 mph.

4. Mercedes-Benz GLS-Class – $100,000

Next comes the Mercedes-GLS Class that Drew Brees has got inside his car collection. The GLS features a stunningly-designed exterior and offers a first-class cabin with plenty of luxury. The GLS packs a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine that makes 483 hp of power. Apart from the incredibly performing engine to its luxury cabin, the SUV is for the highest class of people. From the fine leather-stiched interior, and messaging seats, the GLS got everything.
