Prince Carl Philip, Duke of Vrmland Biography; Net Worth, Wedding, Girlfriends

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  229 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Prince Carl Philip of Sweden officially known as Carl Philip Edmund Bertil was born on May 13, 1979to King Carl XVI Gustaf and Queen Silvia of Sweden. Prince Carl Philip whose royal title is the Duke of Värmland is the second of three children to King Carl XVI and Queen Silvia of Sweden.

Unlike others who had to join long queues to be crowned prince, Prince Carl was the Crown Prince of Sweden from birth as Sweden traditions did not allow women to rule as Queen Regnant. However, his fate changed when Sweden passed the Act of Succession to introduce fully cognate succession changing Prince Carl’s position from crown prince to second in line after his elder sister.

Prince Carl Philip Wedding

The wedding of Prince Carl Philip, Duke of Värmland, and Sofia Hellqvist took place on 13 June 2015 at Slottskyrkan, Stockholm. This followed the couple’s announcement of their engagement on 27 June 2014.
