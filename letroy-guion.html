Career

  • Guion participated in ten games as a backup defensive lineman for Florida State as a true freshman, totaling 10 tackles and one quarterback hurry. During the 2005 season, Guion was the only true freshman to make a defensive start.
  • Guion declared his intention to forego his senior season and enter the 2008 NFL Draft after starting only 14 games for the Seminoles during his three years with the team.
