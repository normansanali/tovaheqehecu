Highlights

  • Sophie Turner goes Instagram official with her new boyfriend Peregrine Pearson, sharing ski trip photos with friends.
  • Sophie's relationship with Peregrine appears to be getting more serious, as she showcases him on social media.
  • Some speculate that Sophie's post was a response to sister-in-law Priyanka Chopra's double date post with Joe Jonas and his girlfriend Stormi Bree, hinting at family dynamics.

Sophie Turner is showing that she’s moving on from the Jonas family after her sister-in-law Priyanka Chopra revealed she’s a fan of Joe Jonas’ new girlfriend Stormi Bree.

Sharing with her 14.4 million Instagram followers, Sophie went Instagram official with her new love interest, British aristocrat Peregrine Pearson. The duo appeared to enjoy a ski trip recently with friends. The Game of Thrones actress shared several snaps of them on IG alongside the other friendly faces in their group.

Sophie Went Skiing With Her New Boyfriend

Sophie was first seen kissing Peregrine last fall after Joe filed for divorce in September. Sophie and Peregrine were spotted spending time together in London, where she’s been shooting the television series Joan.

Though Sophie hasn’t officially commented on her romance with Peregrine (or made it red carpet official), the fact that she’s showcasing him on social media could signify their relationship is getting a bit more serious.

Priyanka Chopra Seemed To Diss Sophie With A Double Date Post

On the other hand, it could’ve been Sophie’s way of indirectly responding to her sister-in-law Priyanka Chopra, who recently appeared to share a glimpse of Joe and Stormi’s budding romance.

On January 22, Priyanka made a post to her Instagram Story on what appeared to be a double date with her husband, Nick Jonas, as well as Joe and Stormi. The four clinked their champagne flutes as they appeared to enjoy dinner at a restaurant.

Stormi – who was first connected to Joe at the start of the new year when they traveled to Mexico together – wasn’t tagged in the post. But Internet sleuths noticed that one of the people in the post had a tattoo that appears identical to the one Stormi known to have on her right hand.

At the time, some critics theorized that Priyanka made the post to subtly diss Sophie and show that the family is welcoming Joe’s new girlfriend, even though his divorce isn’t finalized.

“Priyanka uploaded this story and according to me one is Nick and the other two are a woman and a man,” one person wrote on X (formerly Twitter). “Joe because of the ring, I don’t know if it’s the same one he wears but it’s very similar and Stormi because of the tattoo on her hand.”

“I mean - we all know! Post a picture together already! Give the people what they want!!” another person said, encouraging Joe and Stormi to go Instagram-official, like Sophie recently did with her new romantic interest.

Joe and Sophie met in 2016 and had a whirlwind relationship before announcing their engagement in October 2017. The couple then surprised fans by tying the knot in a private ceremony in Las Vegas in May 2019, followed by a more formal wedding celebration in France in June of the same year. In July 2020, Joe and Sophie welcomed their first child, a daughter named Willa, followed by a second daughter, Delphine, in 2022.
