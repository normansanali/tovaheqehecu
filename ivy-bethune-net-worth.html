Ivy Bethune Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Ivy Bethune was born on June 1, 1918 in Sevastopol, Russia as Ivy Vigder. She is an actress, known for Back to the Future (1985), General Hospital (1963) and Get Smart (2008). She was previously married to Stuart Lancaster and William Charles Bethune. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
