Biography:

Gracie Dzienny was born on August 26, 1995 in Toledo, Ohio, U.S.. She was only five when she began working as a model. During her early years, she also studied different forms of dance, such as tap, jazz, ballet and hip hop. In 2010, she was cast for the role of Amanda McKay on the Nickelodeon show Supah Ninjas. After getting the part, she and her mother moved to Hollywood.

She starred on the said show from 2011 to 2013, gaining recognition. Simultaneously, she guest starred on other TV shows. This includes Up All Night, Fred: The Show, and See Dad Run. She next guest starred in an episode of Side Effects. From 2014 to 2015, she played a recurring role as Greer Danville on Chasing Life. In 2016, she started playing the role of Clementine on Zoo.
