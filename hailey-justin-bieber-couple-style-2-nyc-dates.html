Hailey and Justin Bieber Show Off Couple Style on Two NYC Dates in One Day

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  312 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Hailey and Justin Bieber showed off their street styles while out in New York City yesterday.

The pair first stepped out for a lunch date in matching white T-shirts and later enjoyed a date night in different looks.

Hailey wore her boxy cropped white tee by Leset with cutoff light-wash jean shorts cinched with a black leather belt. She finished the look with black loafers paired with white ankle socks, a woven black shoulder bag by Bottega Veneta, skinny black sunglasses, and gold jewelry, including hoop earrings, a watch, and her diamond engagement ring from Justin.

The “Peaches” singer wore his oversize tee with lavender basketball shorts and white sneakers paired with long white socks. His only accessory was a bright orange Summerland trucker hat.

MEGA//Getty Images

For their second outing of the day, the Rhode founder dressed up in a silky white minidress with a floral jacquard pattern and an asymmetric neckline with a cutout. She styled it with strappy white sandals, her go-to elongated black sunglasses, and gold jewelry, including the same chunky hoops and watch, plus a dainty chain necklace. For glam, she wore a glossy red lip and had her blonde bob styled in a side-parted ’do.

Justin kept it casual in a warm-gray hoodie layered over a white tank top and worn with loose light-wash blue jeans, mustard-yellow Vans sneakers, and a red-and-white checked cap, which he wore backward.

The pair held hands while on their way out of West Village hotspot L’Artusi.
