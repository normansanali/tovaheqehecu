Nathaniel Cline Biography

Nathaniel Cline is an American award-winning Journalist, Photographer and Digital Multimedia Producer who was born and brought-up in Virginia. Currently, he works at Loudoun Time-Mirror in Leesburg, Virginia as a Politics and Governments Reporter, Writer and Editor. He is widely known as the husband of Yamiche Alcindor, a renowned American Journalist and White House Correspondent.

Prior to his current position, he worked at cleveland.com networks including The Washington Post, USA Today, WKYC, and Fox News as a Sports Reporter for over four years. He served as a Sports Editor for Northern Neck News, as a Government Reporter, Photographer and Copy Editor for Westmoreland News, and as an Anchor, Cameraman, Reporter, Director and Writer for Bowie States University.
