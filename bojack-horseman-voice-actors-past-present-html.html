BoJack Horseman, Netflix’s adult animated series about a ‘90s sitcom star turned sad, alcoholic has-been (who also happens to be an anthropomorphic horse) returned for a third season last Friday. As expected, it delivered more shenanigans — both hilarious and deeply depressing — from BoJack and his animal and human cohorts, who are voiced by a star-studded cast including Will Arnett, Amy Sedaris, and Allison Brie. Wondering who’s behind the voices Mr. Peanutbutter’s brother, strip-club owner Goober, and Jessica Biel?
