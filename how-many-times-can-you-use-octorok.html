How many times can you use Octorok?

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  642 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

1. How many times can Octorok repair weapons?

Octorok can only repair one item and do so just once. After that, you will need to find another Octorok or wait for a Blood Moon to respawn them in order to repair more items.

2. Do Octoroks Respawn?

Yes, Octoroks can respawn during a Blood Moon. So if you need to repair multiple items, you can either find multiple Octoroks or wait for a Blood Moon to have them respawn.

3. How do Octoroks work?

To make use of Octorok’s repair abilities, drop your weapon or shield in front of it and run away to a safe distance. The Octorok will gulp up your item and begin repairing it. Once you see sparkles around the Octorok, it will spit the repaired item back towards you.

4. Why is the Octorok not repairing my weapon?

Octoroks can only repair non-unique weapons and shields. If your weapon is unique or has already been repaired by the Octorok, it will not repair it any further.

5. Can Octoroks repair bows?

Yes, Octoroks can repair bows. You can throw a bow at an Octorok and it will repair and spit it back out to you, making it usable again.

6. Do Octoroks get rid of decay?

When an Octorok repairs an item, it fixes the weapon durability but does not remove decay caused by the Upheaval.

7. Can Octoroks fix fused weapons?

Yes, Octoroks can fix fused weapons. The Octorok’s ability to repair weapons and shields includes fused ones as well.

8. Do rock Octoroks remove decay?

No, Octoroks do not remove decay from weapons. They only repair the weapon’s durability, not the decay caused by the Upheaval.

9. Can you repair bows in totk?

Yes, you can repair bows in Tears of the Kingdom by using Octoroks.

10. What do Octoroks drop?

Upon defeat, Octoroks may drop various items such as crabs, fish, Octo Balloons, Octorok Eyeballs, Octorok Tentacles, or snails.

11. Does the Korok sword respawn?

No, the Korok sword does not respawn. Once you obtain the Korok sword, you will not be able to find additional ones.

12. Can Octoroks repair the Hylian Shield?

No, Octoroks cannot repair the Hylian Shield. The Hylian Shield can only be repaired by the Rock Octoroks found around Goron Town.

13. Does the Fierce Deity sword break?

Yes, the Fierce Deity sword can break like any other weapon in the game.

14. Do weapons respawn in totk?

Chests containing weapons do not respawn in Tears of the Kingdom. However, other weapons may respawn over time.

15. Can you fuse the Master Sword?

No, the Master Sword cannot be fused with other items.

16. What are the bonuses for Rock Octorok?

Rock Octoroks can provide various bonuses when repairing weapons, such as Attack Up, Critical Hit, Durability Up, and more.

17. What is the strongest bow in Core Keeper?

The Octarine Bow is considered one of the strongest ranged weapons in Core Keeper, with a DPS of 212-258.

18. Can you infinitely repair a bow?

No, you cannot infinitely repair a bow. Each bow can only be repaired a limited number of times before it becomes unusable.

19. Is there an unbreakable bow in Zelda?

No, there is no unbreakable bow in Zelda. Every bow in the game has limited durability and will eventually break.

20. Does the Octorok repair rusted items?

No, Octoroks do not repair rusted items. They only repair the item’s durability, not its condition.

21. Can you repair the Infinity bow?

Yes, you can repair the Infinity bow, but it can only be repaired a maximum of 5 times before needing a new Infinity bow.

22. Is there an unbreakable sword in Zelda?

Yes, the Master Sword is the only unbreakable sword in Zelda. It does not need repair and can be used indefinitely.
