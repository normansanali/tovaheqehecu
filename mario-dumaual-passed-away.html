Mario Dumaual Passed Away: The Legacy Of A Reporter

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  676 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The entertainment industry mourns the loss of Mario Dumaual, a well-known and respected entertainment reporter. 

On Wednesday, July 5, Mario Dumaual passed away at 64 due to septic shock, the severe stage of sepsis caused by an infection. 

Mario Dumaual’s family statement, shared on Facebook, shed light on his challenges during his final days. 

He was admitted to the Philippine Heart Center for treatment after having a heart attack, but his struggle continued after that.

In addition to the heart attack, he had to fight a difficult fungal infection that made his situation worse.

Despite the stress on his body and mind, Mario persisted in his resolve to overcome these obstacles. 

His rehabilitation was greatly influenced by his desire to be with his cherished family again.

He expressed how much he wanted to return home and be with his family and how deeply and crucially important their relationship was to him.

However, the fungus proved to be a formidable opponent, surpassing his body’s capacity to heal.

Mario’s condition deteriorated despite the best efforts of medical staff and his family’s assistance because of the infection’s overpowering effects. 

Mario and his loved ones experienced devastation as they had hoped for a full recovery and a return to a happy life.

A Rock and a Proud Family Man 

Mario Dumaual’s family paid tribute to him as a loving husband, father, and granddad. 

In addition to his accomplishments as a reporter, Mario nurtured his family, empowering them to become strong individuals. 

His family acknowledged his impact on their lives, crediting him as their rock of support. He made his family proud as Mario Dumaual, the reporter, and as a dedicated family man.

Celebrating Mario’s farewell

The remembrance of Mario Dumaual will take place at the Loyola Memorial Chapels and Crematorium Commonwealth.

Funeral will begin on Thursday and continue until Sunday, starting at 2 pm each day.

During this solemn occasion, friends, colleagues, and fans can unite and show their respect for Mario Dumaual. 

The farewell provides a chance for people to reflect on the life and achievements of Mario Dumaual, celebrating the moments he shared with his audience and the valuable insights he brought to the field of entertainment reporting.

By attending the wake, individuals can offer comfort to Mario’s family and express their condolences. 

Damual’s Career in Journalism

Mario Dumaual’s career in journalism began after graduating from the University of the Philippines Los Banos. 

He started as a staff writer for the Ministry of Agriculture and subsequently worked in the finance and shipping sectors.

In 1982, Mario joined the Times Journal as an entertainment reporter, where he showcased his talents and passion for reporting on celebrities, award shows, pageants, and galas.

He later became a prominent writer for the entertainment columns of the broadsheet newspaper Malaya.

A Mentor and an Inspiration

During his tenure at ABS-CBN, Mario Dumaual made a significant impact as a reporter and as a mentor to Star Patrol reporters. 

His guidance and expertise helped shape the careers of aspiring journalists in the field of entertainment reporting. 

Mario’s influence extended beyond his professional life, as his son Miguel followed in his footsteps, joining ABS-CBN News Digital as a TV reporter and editor.

A Star Remembered

Condolences send from all corners of the entertainment industry as news after Mario Dumaual passed away.

Friends, family, and admirers sent their condolences to Mario’s family and expressed their deep sorrow.

Celebrities and other journalists praised Mario’s professionalism, morality, and commitment to his work.

They praised his ability to tell captivating stories and his commitment to bringing his audience the latest news and insights. 

Mario’s friendly demeanor and sincere interactions deeply impacted many people, making his loss even more difficult to bear.

Fans also expressed their sorrow over the passing of someone they thought was essential to the entertainment industry.

They highlighted Mario’s positive influence on their lives through his reporting or his genuine connection with his audience. 

His passion for his work and ability to bring stories to life resonated with many, and his absence will be deeply felt.
