Who Is Justin Herbert?

Justin Patrick Herbert, born on March 10, 1998, is a talented American football quarterback, currently playing for the Los Angeles Chargers in the NFL. Before making a name for himself in the professional league, Herbert showcased his skills at the University of Oregon, where he held various records, including most passing yards, most passing touchdowns, and most total touchdowns.

In 2020, the Los Angeles Chargers picked Herbert as the sixth overall selection in the NFL Draft. Remarkably, in his rookie season, he set multiple records for a rookie quarterback, earning him the prestigious title of NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year.

In his second season, Herbert continued to impress, consistently throwing for over 4,000 yards and 30 touchdowns each year. His exceptional performance has solidified his position as one of the NFL’s brightest young quarterbacks.
