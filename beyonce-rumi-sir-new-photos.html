Beyonc Shares New Photos of Twins Rumi and Sir for Birthday

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  291 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
  • To commemorate her 38th birthday, superstar Beyoncé shared never-before-seen snaps on her website, taken from her personal photo album.
  • The singer shared several new photos of her children: adorable twins Rumi and Sir, and style icon Blue Ivy.
  • Zoë Kravitz was overjoyed to find out that Beyoncé had dressed up as her mom, Lisa Bonet, for Halloween.

Beyoncé decided to give fans a behind-the-scenes look at her family life in honor of her 38th birthday.

Related Story

As well as sharing a set of photos taken at Blue Ivy's seventh birthday party, which had a chic rose gold theme, the singer also gave fans a glimpse of baby twins Rumi and Sir.

Snapped at sister Blue Ivy's party, both Rumi and Sir looked sweet while spending time with Mom. Rumi was pictured smiling as she stood up by herself, with help of a chair, and Sir was lifted into the air by Beyoncé. Both children are growing up so fast.

For Halloween, the superstar revealed that she dressed up as '90s icon and actress Lisa Bonet. Rumi and Sir became the perfect accessories for the costume, as Beyoncé dressed them both to look like a baby Zoë Kravitz, Bonet's daughter with Lenny Kravitz. Needless to say, Beyoncé won Halloween.

Beyonce.com

Fans will be excited to see Beyoncé's photos of Rumi and Sir, especially as the twins are usually kept out of the spotlight. Head over to beyonce.com to see even more behind-the-scenes snaps from the singer.
