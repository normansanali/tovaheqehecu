What is the best repellent for lizards?

Pepper spray to keep them away

To get rid of lizards and prevent them from returning, pepper water is a safe-to-use trick. Mix some black pepper powder in water and spray the solution into anywhere that you usually find the offending pests.

What are house lizards afraid of?

What home remedy kills lizards?

Coffee Powder

Why do lizards come into the house?

Lizards and geckos are most likely appearing in the house because they can easily find food inside. In most cases, food will be small insects like ants, roaches, and beetles. If you get rid of the household pests then the population of lizards inside the house will gradually disappear.
