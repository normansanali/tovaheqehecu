AEW Dynamite Results Coverage, Reactions &amp; Highlights For November 22, 2023

It’s Wednesday night, and you know what that means! AEW Dynamite will go down tonight, and Ringside News has got you covered with live play-by-play results coverage for the November 22, 2023 episode.

The start time for AEW Dynamite is 8:00 PM EST. Match-by-match highlights and results from the show will take place on this very page. Feel free to leave any reactions in the comment section below.

Tonight promises another exciting night of action as we get all the fallout from Full Gear. Plus, the Continental Classic Tournament kicks off tonight on Dynamite.

AEW Dynamite Lineup (11/22/2023):

  • Orange Cassidy, Hook, & Katsuyori Shibata vs. Daddy Magic, Cool Hand Ang, & Jake Hager
  • Ruby Soho vs. Skye Blue vs. Anna Jay
  • Christian Cage will rechristen Luchasaurus and Nick Wayne
  • Continental Classic Gold League: Jon Moxley vs. Mark Briscoe
  • Continental Classic Gold League: Swerve Strickland vs. Jay Lethal
  • Continental Classic Gold League: Rush vs. Jay White
  • Toni Storm’s Championship Acceptance Speech

AEW Dynamite Results (11/22/2023):

The opening package for the show airs.

Continental Classic Gold League: Swerve Strickland vs. Jay Lethal

Swerve Strickland’s music plays and he walks to the ring. Jay Lethal’s theme song plays and he comes down to the ring.

The match begins and Lethal knocks down Strickland. Swerve with an hurricanrana sending Lethal out of the ring. Both men exchange strikes on the apron. Swerve is knocked off the apron. Lethal with a suicide dive onto Swerve.

Lethal sends Swerve into the ring and goes to work on his arm. Lethal with a lariat. Swerve comes back with a basement dropkick. He attacks Lethal in the corner. Swerve goes to work on the leg of Lethal.

Lethal comes back and targets the arm of Swerve. Swerve kicks Lethal in the leg. Both men exchange strikes. Swerve with a German suplex. Lethal comes back with a shoulder beaker.

*Commercial break*

Swerve hits a superplex. Swerve with a flatliner and he follows it with a brainbuster. Cover! 1…..2……kick out. Lethal comes back with a reverse suplex. He climbs the top rope and hits the elbow drop. Cover! 1…2…kick out. Letha goes for a dropkick and misses. Swerve with the stretch muffler.

Lethal goes for Lethal injection and Swerve reverses it and plants him. He hits the house call and follows with the Swerve stomp for the win.

Winner: Swerve Strickland

Renee interviews Orange Cassidy, Hook, and Shibata. Orange is interrupted by Wheeler Yuta who congratulates him on his win against Jon Moxley. He says he hasn’t forgotten about Shibata. He wishes him luck for his title match tomorrow but he’s gonna win back the title. Yuta walks away.

Better Than You Bay Bay’s music plays and they walk to the ring. MJF gets on the mic and says he beat an undefeated Jay White with one leg. He says Jay is one of the best professional wrestlers in the world but nobody is on his level. He says he has earned the right to toot his own horn.

MJF calls himself the greatest AEW World Champion of all time. He says none of that was possible without Adam Cole. Cole says it is great to be back in Chicago. He says it is also great that they are the ROH Tag Team Champions. He then says his ankle is in bad shape and he isn’t close to walking yet. He promises to work hard everyday to get back as quickly as possible.

Cole says he is worried about MJF because he can’t be near his side. MJF says he doesn’t have to worry about him. He says he is going to find the person behind his devil mask. The lights go out and the person in the devil mask appears on the titantron and laughs.

Samoa Joe’s music plays and he walks to the ring. Joe says there are a lot of concerned faces in the ring when they should be celebrating. He says MJF defended the ROH Tag Titles with him for the simple price of offering him a World Title match. MJF asks him to take a step back. He says he has a mature response to his rematch request and he rejects the request. Joe catches MJF by the collar. Cole pleads with Joe to let go of him and he asks MJF to honor his deal.

Cole says the fans love MJF because when he says something is going to happen, it happens. He asks him to be a man of his word. MJF challenges him to a title match right here and now. Joe turns him down. He says MJF is going to complain if he loses tonight. He says he wants the best version of MJF. Joe says they should have their match by World’s End. MFJ accepts the match for World’s End.

MJF says he is almost impossible to beat in Long Island. Joe says MJF is his property from now on and he will be a few steps behind and he is going to deliver the very best MJF at World’s End before taking his World Title.

*Commercial break*

Orange Cassidy, Hook, & Katsuyori Shibata vs. Daddy Magic, Cool Hand Ang, & Jake Hager

Hook’s music plays and he walks out. Katsuyori Shibata’s theme song plays and he walks out. Orange Cassidy’s music plays and he joins them and they walk to the ring. Their opponents are in the ring.

Orange gets on the mic and says he has a Thanksgiving surprise. Danhausen’s music plays and he comes down to the ring.

The match begins and Daddy Magic attacks Hook in the corner. He comes back and takes Daddy Magic down. Hager attacks Hook from behind. Double team from Angelo Parker and Daddy Magic. Daddy Magic tags out and Hager hits the Hager bomb. Cover! 1…..2….kick out.

*Commercial break*

Hook comes back with a T-Bone suplex. He tags out. Cassidy attacks Daddy Magic and Hager. He hits DDT on Menard. Hager slams Cassidy. Cassidy with a stundog millionaire. He tags out and Shibata attacks Hager. Shibata with a pump kick. He attacks Hager in the corner. Shibata with a running dropkick. Menard and Parker attack Shibata from behind.

Danhausen is on the apron and he has Hager’s hat. Hager goes after him. Danhausen curses him and Cassidy hits the suicide dive. Hook is tagged in. Hook locks in the red rum on Parker while Shibata locks in the sleeper hold on Menard. Cassidy with an Orange punch to Hager as Parker taps out.

Winner: Orange Cassidy, Hook, & Katsuyori Shibata

Renee interviews Adam Cole who says he can’t wait to get back in the ring and he is proud of MJF for sticking to his word. Roderick Strong interrupts him and asks where he was when Action Andretti dropped him on his head. Cole says he is not Strong’s best friend anymore and asks him to back off and leave him alone.

*Commercial break*

Christian Cage’s music plays and he walks out to the ring with Luchasaurus and Nick Wayne. Cage says everybody knows a true champion is gracious in victory and humble in defeat. He says Full Gear didn’t go his way. He says he did not lose that match. He says Nick Wayne did not lose that match. He then says Luchasaurus lost that match and it’s unacceptable.

He says Wayne and Luchasaurus’ life have gotten better since they were associated with him and it’s time to recreate them in his image. He asks Luchasaurus to take a knee. Luchasaurus doesn’t do it and he repeats himself. He gets on his knee. He says the name Luchasaurus will be associated with being a loser. He says he came up of a name that means toughness. He calls him his finisher and calls him Killswitch.

Nick gets on his knee but Cage asks him to get up and tells him not to get on his feet for anyone as he is special. He calls him his golden boy. He says he will be known as the Prodigy. They hug in the ring. Nick Wayne’s mother comes down to the ring and pleads with her son.

Cage asks what is she doing here. He calls her a terrible mother. He makes fun of her for being a waitress. He says it is a good thing that Buddy Wayne is dead because Nick would’ve disowned him. Cage says things could’ve been different if she played her cards right and he asks her to get out of the ring. KIllswitch gets in Cage’s face. He asks him to get on his knees. Killswitch doesn’t move and Cage slaps him. Cage asks him to back down and shoves him and he knocks down Nick Wayne’s mom.

Nick Wayne grabs a steel chair from under the ring and sends it into the ring. He grabs another chair from under the ring. Cage gives the steel chair to Killswitch and asks him to hit her with the chair. Adam Copeland’s music plays and he comes down to the ring and spears Wayne. He knocks Killswitch out of the ring. Killswitch drags Cage out of the ring. Copeland spears Wayne and hits the impaler on him.

Copeland his Wayne with the con-chair-to. Renee interviews Anna Jay. Menard yells that he doesn’t know what is going on. Jay says this is about her. She says she wants to wrestle and she is tired of them. She says she is going to hurt someone.

Continental Classic Gold League: Rush vs. Jay White

Rush’s music plays and he comes out with his LFI stablemates. Jay White’s music plays and he walks out to the ring with The Gunns. The referee informs Jay White that The Gunns have to leave. The Gunns leave the ring.

The match starts and Rush backs White into the corner. White leaves the ring. He gets back in the ring but Rush hits the dropkick. Rush stomps him. Rush attacks him in the corner and hits a dropkick. White rolls out of the ring. Rush goes after him and chops him. Rush sends White into the barricade. He does it again multiple times. Rush chops White. He sends White into the ring. White attacks Rush in the corner. Rush turns the tables and attacks White in the corner.

*Commercial break*

Rush knocks down Jay White. He hits the three amigos. Cover! 1….2….kick out. Rush goes for a dropkick and misses. White hits the brainbuster. Cover! 1….2….kick out. White chops Rush. Both men exchange strikes. White hits the urinagi. Cover! 1…..2…..kick out.

Both men exchange strikes. Rush hits a German suplex. White comes back with a dragon screw. Rush throws White into the turnbuckle. Rush with an elbow strike. White hits the low blow while the referee doesn’t see. He hits the bladerunner for the win.

Winner: Jay White

*Commercial break*

RJ City and Renee Paquette come out. RJ City says they are here to present the AEW Women’s Champion. They announce that the title goes to Toni Storm. Toni Storm’s music plays and she walks to the entrance stage. Mariah May presents the title to her. Toni says she didn’t have anything prepared.

She pulls out a piece of paper and thanks Tony Khan and Warner Discover. Her music plays again and she says she is not done. She says she wants to thank the little fans who want to become her but they can’t take her spot. Her music plays again and she celebrates with the title.

Ruby Soho vs. Skye Blue vs. Anna Jay

Skye Blue’s music plays and she comes out to the ring.

*Commercial break*

Anna Jay’s music plays and he she comes out to the ring. Ruby Soho’s theme song plays and she comes out with Saraya.

The match begins and all three women brawl. Skye and Anna superkick Soho. Skye knocks down Anna. Ruby hits a side suplex on Skye. Skye knocks Soho out of the ring. Anna knocks Skye out of the ring.

*Commercial break*

Skye fights off Soho and Anna. She knocks down Anna with a lariat. Skye goes for a baseball dropkick but Soho sends her into the barricade. Parker and Soho stare at each other. Anna kicks her and sends her into the ring. Skye with a crossbody on them. She goes for code Blue but Anna reverses and plants her. Cover! 1…..2…..Soho makes the save. Soho sends Ann out of the ring. Menard catches the leg of Soho, Skye with a pump kick on Soho and she falls into Parker’s hand. Skye hits the cutter on Anna for the win.

Winner: Skye Blue

Renee interviews Wardlow who says the world doesn’t revolve around MJF and his downfall is going to be on him. AR Fox comes in and advices Wardlow who says he doesn’t need his advice. Wardlow headbutts Fox and walks away.

Continental Classic Gold League: Jon Moxley vs. Mark Briscoe

Mark Briscoe’s music plays and he walks to the ring. Jon Moxley’s music hits and he makes his way to the ring.

The match begins and both men exchange strikes. Moxley attacks him in the corner. Mark sends Moxley out of the ring and hits a suicide dive. He hits a neckbreaker. Mark sends Moxley in the ring. Moxley attacks Jay in the corner and knocks him out of the ring.

*Commercial break*

Moxley with a piledriver. Cover! 1….2….kick out. Mark knocks down Moxley. Briscoe with the exploder suplex. Cover! 1….2…..kick out. Moxley locks in the sleeper and then transitions into the armbar. Briscoe gets to the rope. Mark climbs the top rope but is stopped. He knocks Moxley off the top rope. Briscoe goes for an axehandle but Moxley hits the paradigm shift and follows it with a dropkick.

Mark with the Death Valley driver. He climbs the top rope and hits the Froggie bow. Cover! 1…..2….kick out. Mark hits him with a lariat. Moxley comes back with the king Kon lariat and follows it with the death rider. Cover! 1….2….kick out. Moxley locks in the sleeper hold. Briscoe gets out of it. Moxley hits the half-and-half suplex.

Both men exchange strikes. Moxley hits the stomp and follows it with the death rider for the win.

Winner: Jon Moxley
