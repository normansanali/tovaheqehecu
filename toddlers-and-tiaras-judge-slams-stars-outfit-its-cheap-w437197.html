Toddlers and Tiaras Judge Slams Stars Outfit: Its Cheap

 Posted on July 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  267 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Sparing too much expense? Aspiring beauty queen Lilly gets a mixture of feedback after her jungle pageant performance in Toddlers and Tiaras’ Wednesday, August 31, episode, as seen in Us Weekly’s exclusive sneak peek.

The TLC series’ preview clip shows her grandmother encouraging her before the big competition: “You know what to do. I know you’ve worked hard, you hear me?”

Just before she makes her way onto the stage, 4-year-old Lilly reveals her confidence to the camera. “I never get nervous,” the youngster boasts.

As Lilly poses and struts for the crowd, the announcer says, “Lilly’s favorite food is chicken nuggets and sauce — lots of sauce. Her ambition is to be a big star one day. … Lilly’s hobbies include four-wheel, dance and gymnastics.”

Her coach, Cambrie Littlefield, is certainly proud of her pupil’s performance. “Lilly’s personality for beauty was, like, on point,” Littlefield tells the camera. “She had so much fun — she looked gorgeous. I mean, she killed it.”

Unfortunately, judge Chloe Brown is decidedly less impressed. “Lilly’s fabric was a little bit cheap,” Brown says in an interview. “Maybe for this particular pageant, [the family] could have invested in something a little more expensive. Just, I didn’t like it very much.”

A preview clip from last week’s episode featured Lilly’s mom, Amber, inexplicably breaking down in tears when her daughter portrayed a “happy monkey.” Amber added about her bundle of joy, “I’m just so proud of her.”
