    Personal Trainer

    Towards the end of the summer of 2017, Remi decided to kickstart a weight loss regime as she wanted to take care of her health. She wanted to improve her cholesterol levels and blood pressure and was worried that diabetes, which runs in her family, might affect her if she didn’t overhaul her lifestyle.

    As she wasn’t a fan of traditional exercises such as running, she sought alternatives and found a perfect option in SoulCycle. And, by sticking to the SoulCycle routine, she managed to lose about 45 pounds in a space of three months.

    She has also made changes to her diet. But she allows herself to manage it by day to day basis and allows some room for her cravings. If she indulges her cravings one day, she makes sure that she is following a healthier diet the following day.

    Her daily diet plan is as follows –

    • Morning Ritual – She likes to start her day with lemon water and hot herbal tea.
    • Breakfast – For her first meal of the day, she likes to have turkey egg scramble made with egg whites, avocado, cherry tomatoes, extra-lean turkey mince, and buffalo sauce.
    • Lunch – She likes to have salmon sashimi with roasted vegetables, ginger, and ponzu sauce for lunch.
    • Dinner – She likes to end her day with a potluck of whatever is there in her fridge. But she keeps it light.
    • Snacking – Pita chips with hummus, protein bars, hard-boiled eggs, and almonds.
