Thomas Earle’s Background

Thomas was born to his parents, Walter Earle Sr. and Marianne Dryer Earle, in January 1973. His parents were together for over four decades until his father died in 2004 at 67.

Thomas Earle is the CEO of Earle, a well-established road construction and paving company based in New Jersey. With a legacy spanning 54 years, the company holds a unique tradition inspired by the events of September 11: each piece of its construction equipment proudly displays the American flag, symbolizing its unwavering patriotism.

Beyond their business endeavors, the Earle family is actively involved in philanthropy. They operate a foundation with a primary focus on combating the devastating impact of opioid addiction, addressing an issue of great importance in their community.

Thomas’s extensive experience in the industry is evident through his leadership roles. He has served as the president of the New Jersey Asphalt Pavement Association for three terms and currently holds the treasurer position. Additionally, Thomas has made significant contributions to the National Asphalt Pavement Association, having served on its Board of Directors and Executive Committee from 2002 to 2010.
