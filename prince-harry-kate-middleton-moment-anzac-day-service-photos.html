Prince Harry &amp; Kate Middleton's Joint Anzac Day Appearance Had the Sweetest Moment

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
  • Kate Middleton and Prince Harry stepped out together on April 25, 2019 to attend an Anzac Day service at Westminster Abbey.
  • Harry and Kate dispelled any rumors of a feud with their joint attendance and shared some seriously sweet moments at the service.
  • Harry's attendance was only revealed just before the service, as wife Meghan Markle's due date is rumored to arrive very soon.

The royals have been plagued by feud rumors since it was revealed that Prince Harry and Meghan Markle would be leaving Kensington Palace for Frogmore Cottage in Windsor.

Related Story

However, the Duke and Duchess of Sussex have now moved, and Prince William and Kate Middleton have already visited the couple's new home as the royals begin to settle in.

Stepping out together to attend an Anzac Day service, in honor of Australians and New Zealanders who have died during war and conflict, Harry and Kate put any Kensington Palace split rumors to bed. Plus, they managed to have some exceedingly adorable moments together that you might have missed, proving that their friendship as in-laws is well and truly intact.

In one particularly sweet moment, Harry could be seen gazing at Kate, and there was so much warmth in his expression, it's clear that they're still close:

WPA Pool//Getty Images

And as the gentleman that he is, Prince Harry was also seen motioning for his sister-in-law to walk ahead of him as they left the service.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

Basically, rumors of a feud between Harry and Meghan, and Kate and William, appear to have been fabricated. The royals are just fine, thank you very much.
