Askar Askarov’s net worth is reported to be increasing as he has been excelling in his career as an MMA fighter. 

Askar Askarov is a Mixed Martial Artist and Freestyle Wrestler. Currently, he is known to be competing in the Flyweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC).

Furthermore, Askar has also competed in Absolute Championship Berkut (ACB). He won the ACB Flyweight Championship and is the 2017 Summer Deaflympics 61kg freestyle wrestling gold medalist.

Askar Askarov Net Worth 2022 – How Much?

According to Tapology, Askar Askarov’s net worth is estimated to be $56K USD.

The major source of Askar’s income is his career in athletics. He had made his debut on the mani card of UFC Fight Night: Rodríguez vs. Stephens against Brandon Moreno.

At the end of the match, they split the draw. Askar was competing in front of his home fans in Mexico then.

On 18th January 2020, Askarov was booked to face former UFC flyweight title challenger Tim Elliott. It was his second fight. In Askar’s favour, he won the bout via unanimous decision.

Later on 19th July 2020, for his third fight, Askar faced Alexandre Pantoja at UFC Fight Night 172. In this match too Askar won via unanimous decision.

Likewise, he faced Joseph Benavidez at UFC 259 on 6th March 2021. However, Askar was fined 20 per cent of his individual purse as his bout proceeded at a catchweight because he was one pound over the flyweight non-title fight limit.

The MMA fighter’s recent match was on 26th March 2022 at UFC Fight Night 205 where he faced Kai Kara-France. Unfortunately, he lost the first bout of his professional career via unanimous decision.

Askar Askarov Family & Religion Details

There is little to no information about Askar Askarov’s family available on the internet as of now. The MMA fighter has not mentioned anything about his family members in any interviews.

Likewise, he has remained tight-lipped about his religion as well. However, we know that he holds Russian citizenship.

Askar was born on 9th October 1992 in Kamysh-Kutan, Akhvakhsky District, Republic of Dagestan, Russia. His native name is Аскар Сайпулаевич Аскаров.

Furthermore, the MMA fighter was born deaf. However, it is reported that his hearing has improved. He can hear 20% of the sounds most people can. This means that he cannot hear instructions from his team during a fight.
