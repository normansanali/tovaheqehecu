Who is Phil Spencer's wife Fiona?

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  253 words  |  Aldo Pusey

PHIL SPENCER is best known as the charming TV star from Location, Location, Location.

But away from his day job of selling houses, he enjoys his family life with wife Fiona.

Who is Phil Spencer’s wife Fiona?

Not much is known about Phil’s wife Fiona.

We do know however that she is Australian, and they met around 1995.

Phil is from Littlebourne in Kent and is well known on TV.

He is believed to have a fortune of around £10million.

When did Phil and Fiona Spencer get married and do they have kids?

After persuading Fiona to move to England from Oz, the pair got married in 2001.

The couple have two sons together, Jake and Ben.

Has Phil Spencer ever dated Kirstie Allsopp?

Kirstie and Phil have worked together for over two decades, but there is no romance there, and they are NOT a couple.

The Location, Location, Location and Love It or List It pair are so close that Phil refers to Kirstie as his “other wife” and say his kids are used to having a secondary figure around the house.

Despite their quick banter and heartwarming relationship, Phil has insisted there is nothing but a “natural, platonic relationship” between them, while Kirstie has been keen to make clear that “he’s a really, really nice person”, and has said they were too much like brother and sister to ever be romantically involved.

The pair met for a screen test for the show and found they had a natural connection, which has led them to become firm friends.
