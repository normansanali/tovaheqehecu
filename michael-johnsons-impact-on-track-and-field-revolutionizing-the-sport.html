Michael Johnson's Impact on Track and Field: Revolutionizing the Sport

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  517 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

By Jocelyn Alano June 28, 2024 17:25

credit. SportsPulse USA Today

As a legendary figure in the world of track and field, Michael Johnson's influence extends far beyond his four Olympic gold medals and numerous world records. His recent endeavor, the Grand Slam Track (GST), has caught the attention of the global track community and is poised to revolutionize the sport. Let's delve into the impact of Michael Johnson on track and field, including his new venture and the potential changes it brings to the sport. 


The Legacy of Michael Johnson

Michael Johnson is considered one of the greatest long-sprint runners in history and has four Olympic gold medals, as well as multiple world records to prove it. His dominance over both the 200m and 400m events stamps him as one of the best sprinters in history.


Influence Beyond the Track

Despite his retirement, Johnson continued to be a leading voice on the sporting scene. His insistence that track and field evolve, change the narrative, and do things more "bigly" is well-documented; his desire for growth - consistent growth at almost any cost - sometimes can't help but puddle onto every sentence he produces.


The Grand Slam Track

Michael Johnson has launched Grand Slam Track (GST) a global professional track competition starting April 2025. If it comes to fruition, this could be set to redefine the track scene and raise horse racing's profile as a worldwide sport.


Unprecedented Prize Money

The biggest thing in GST, a point that is very important to athletes - it has an enormous value in terms of prize money. GST has now set the benchmark for track and field financial rewards, with $12.6 million (AUD) in prize money up for grabs within this 19-event series, every year.


Revolutionizing Race Formats

GST is expected to provide new point-based competition, which will make track events much more interesting than before. Racers race twice throughout the three-day event, leading to new rivalries and increased interest from fans.


Athlete Empowerment

The way GST goes about athlete sponsorship distinguishes itself from any other professional track meet. Without pitting one against the other, GST provides a rare amount of flexibility for athletes to combine their sponsors and design individual kits allowing them to show more personality on -the field unlike cutting deals with shoe companies.


Support from World Athletics

The global track and field community has welcomed the introduction of GST. World Athletics, which oversees track and field competition worldwide has indicated it is backing Johnson's new athletics league in part because the sport should appeal to investors after years of doping allegations.


In the End

Michael Johnson's legacy in track and field lives on with his innovative way of progressing the sport. Through Grand Slam Track, Johnson is not only redefining what professional track competition looks like but also aiming to rewrite the script on how we value and fete athletes in the sport. With GST set to revolutionize track and field, it's this vision of how Johnson wants the future of running to look: full-on entertainment; a chance for everyone to have their sprinter moment.
