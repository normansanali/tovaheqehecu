Does George Foreman Still Own the Foreman Grill?

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  461 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

By Keith Mado July 10, 2024 21:32

Photo by Anthony Harvey/Getty Images

George Foreman Grill, originally called the George Foreman Lean Mean Fat-Reducing Grilling Machine, was launched in 1994. It's a portable double-sided electrically heated grill that became highly successful because it was so different from any product on the market and also due to an aggressive infomercial campaign featuring legendary two-time world heavyweight boxing champion George Foreman. George Foreman owned the patent for this grill and was instrumental in its creation and promotion.

George Foreman Grill

The American company Salton, Inc. started producing the grill after purchasing all facilities of the original manufacturer; they licensed George Foreman's name and paid him $137.5 million in cash and stock for which to continue selling products bearing his moniker on them, including indoor as well as outdoor grills in 1999. The deal meant Foreman would earn at least $250 million, higher than his boxing career combined. As a result, it is fair to say that George Foreman no longer owns the brand of his beloved grill in recent information available.

The Foreman Grill Today

The Foreman Grill has been a staple product for many years, with over 100 million units sold since its introduction. The product's success was mainly due to its new concept, communications, and George Foreman's recommendation. The Foreman Grill has continued to evolve and improve. For example, in 2006, it released the "Next Grilleration" Health Grill in its current form, the Next Grilleration G5 interchangeable plate grill. This helped keep the device up to date with growing interest and demand in indoor grilling.

The Foreman Grill is among the top indoor grill brands available. What has added to its longevity and sales over time is not only its unique design but also how easy it was to use, along with a key endorsement by George Foreman. Its capability to draw fat from the cooking process has also struck a chord with consumers, which adds an additional layer of depth to its place within the market.

Future of the Foreman Grill

The staying power of indoor grills and the likely longevity of the Foreman Grill brand in that space. Although no immediate new expansion or ownership changes have been reported, Foreman Grill is entrenched in its market, as evidenced by continuing sales. With its track record of success and timeless product for consumers, there is much life left in this iconic indoor grill brand.

Although nothing is definite until George Foreman himself officially announces that he owns another brand and sells just as well, the continued popularity and sales of the grill suggest otherwise. Given the success, design, and strength of presence in this indoor grill marketplace that Foreman Grill has garnered, this is a perfect indication of all its prospects.
