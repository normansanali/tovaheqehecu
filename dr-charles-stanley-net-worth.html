Early Life and Education

Dr. Charles Stanley, a prominent figure in Christian ministry, was born on September 25, 1932, in Dry Fork, Virginia. From an early age, he had a deep spiritual connection and became a born-again Christian at the tender age of 12.

At just 14 years old, Dr. Stanley embarked on his journey in Christian ministry, dedicating his life to serving God and spreading the message of faith. This early passion laid the foundation for his future endeavors and influence in the religious community.

Dr. Stanley prioritized his education and sought formal training to deepen his understanding of theology. He obtained his undergraduate degree from the esteemed University of Richmond, where he honed his intellectual and analytical skills.

Driven by a thirst for knowledge and a desire to enhance his spiritual leadership, Dr. Stanley pursued further education at Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary and Luther Rice Seminary. These academic institutions allowed him to expand his theological knowledge and develop a strong foundation for his future ministry.

His commitment to education led to the attainment of master’s and doctorate degrees in theology, which equipped him with the insight and expertise to connect with his congregation and effectively communicate biblical principles.

Career and Achievements

Dr. Charles Stanley embarked on a remarkable career in ministry that began in 1956. He started as a pastor at a church in Florida and subsequently served in various churches before joining First Baptist Church in Atlanta in 1969. Notably, he assumed the esteemed position of senior pastor in 1971, further solidifying his influence and impact on his congregation and the community.

Throughout his career, Dr. Charles Stanley’s preaching and teaching style captivated audiences, leading to his widespread popularity. He created a religious program called The Chapel Hour, which transcended regional boundaries to reach a nationwide audience hungry for spiritual guidance.

In 1982, Dr. Stanley founded In Touch Ministries, an organization committed to spreading the message of the Gospel through various media platforms. Under his guidance, In Touch Ministries has achieved global recognition and continues to inspire millions of individuals worldwide.

In Touch Ministries

In Touch Ministries, founded by Dr. Charles Stanley in 1982, is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the Christian faith and helping individuals grow spiritually. The ministry produces and distributes various forms of media and resources to reach a wide audience.

One of the flagship programs of In Touch Ministries is In Touch with Dr. Charles Stanley, a radio and TV program that reaches people around the world. It is broadcasted in 50 languages on over 500 radio stations and 300 TV stations, making it accessible to diverse cultures and regions.

In addition to broadcasting, In Touch Ministries publishes a free monthly magazine, In Touch, which offers insightful articles on faith, personal growth, and Christian living. The magazine serves as a valuable resource for individuals seeking to deepen their understanding of biblical principles and apply them to their daily lives.

In Touch Ministries also provides online resources, where users can access a wide range of teachings, sermons, devotionals, and study materials. These resources are designed to empower individuals to study the Bible independently and grow in their relationship with God.
